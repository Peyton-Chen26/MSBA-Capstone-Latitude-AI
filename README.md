# MSBA-Capstone-Latitude-AI
Masters of Business Analytics Capstone Project at Carnegie Mellon University Tepper School of Business with sponsor Latitude AI. 

This project created a proof-of-concept Large Language Model that supports the development of Advanced Driver Assistance Systems for L2 autonomous vehicles and investigates how Large Language Models (LLMs) can be applied to support the development of Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) for Level 2 autonomous vehicles. We explore the utility of LLMs in navigating the ever-evolving legal landscape of the autonomous driving industry. By leveraging LLMs to translate complex regulations into actionable behavioral requirements, we aim to minimize non-compliance risks for Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS).

The proof-of-concept LLM utilizes all information in the “Database” folder: The PDF files for academic mode and the TXT files for legal mode. More information about the data sources can be found in section "2.1.2 Data Collection" of the Final Report.
